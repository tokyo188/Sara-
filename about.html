About Smart Aid and Relief Alliance

Smart Aid and Relief Alliance (SARA) is a platform designed to connect donors, volunteers, and victims during disasters. Our mission is to streamline the process of finding, donating, and requesting resources in times of need.

  • Donors can post available resources and track their impact.
  • Victims can request urgent help and find available resources.
  • Volunteers can coordinate and fulfill requests efficiently.

Together, we make disaster response smarter and more effective.
